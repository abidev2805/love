For the one I belong to
I love holding your hands, the feel of warmth being transferred, the blood rushing through your veins and the beautiful sound of your heart beat drives me crazy.
People say you only fall in love once, but I fall in love all over again every time I see your face, your smile, hear your voice………...
You’re the owner of the most beautiful eyes which makes me fall for you, make my heart skip a beat, the place where I want to live and the eyes which doesn’t need any make up.
Every romantic song, every sweet saying, every love story they all remind of my beautiful small girl.
You’re the everything I want, everything I need, and everything I asked for in my life. Losing is my biggest fear.
The face of a bright sun which shines in the sky, eyes of the shooting star which pierce my heart, smile of a baby which makes me smile and these make me feel like the world’s luckiest person.
